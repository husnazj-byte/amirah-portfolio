Network Infrastructure – Monitoring, protecting, and engineering proactive perimeter defense baselines for organizational systems.

🛡️ Cybersecurity Analyst

A Cybersecurity Analyst plays a vital role in defending an organization's digital domain by actively monitoring networks for vulnerabilities, researching incoming threats, and deploying layered countermeasures. This career position directly interfaces infrastructure design with deep-packet inspection and digital incident management.

Core Operational Responsibilities:
  • Active Perimeter Defense: Continuously monitor enterprise network traffic baselines to catch anomalies, investigate suspicious logs, and enforce firewalls or IDS/IPS rule sets.
  • Vulnerability & Testing: Conduct regular assessments and automated internal penetration testing to catch data flaws before malicious entities find a loophole.
  • Incident Response & Forensics: Isolate, audit, and systematically neutralize perimeter intrusions in real-time, followed by precise cryptographic evidence collection.
Key Technical Competencies Required
Wireshark / Packet Analysis
VPC Flow Logs Visualization
Firewall & ACL Design
Vulnerability Assessment
FastAPI & Python Scripting
Grafana Dashboards
